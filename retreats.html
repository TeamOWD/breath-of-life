The container

What's a Breath of Life retreat?

A small, intentional community gathered on the island of Maui for five nights of conscious connected breathwork, Internal Family Systems (IFS), nature, movement, adventure, and meaningful connection.

This isn't just a vacation. It's a container for transformation.

Through daily breathwork journeys, IFS-informed workshops and group processes, ocean swims, waterfall hikes, nourishing meals, and time in nature, participants are invited to slow down, reconnect with themselves, and cultivate greater compassion for the parts of them that have been carrying burdens for far too long.

As the week unfolds, breathwork helps open the door to deeper awareness, while IFS provides a framework for understanding and healing the protective patterns, inner conflicts, and wounded parts that often keep us stuck. Together, these practices create a powerful pathway toward greater presence, self-leadership, and emotional freedom.

Whether you're completely new to this work or have years of experience, you'll find a welcoming community and an experience designed to support lasting growth, integration, and connection.

The team

Meet the facilitators.

Nick TNT

Breathwork + recovery

Nick TNT is a breathwork and retreat facilitator with over a decade of experience in wellness and recovery. He specializes in conscious connected breathwork to support emotional release, nervous-system regulation, and reconnection to self-trust and purpose.

He is the founder of Breath of Life Wellness and best-selling author of Breath of Life: Finding Long-Term Recovery with Breathwork and Stepwork. Nick's facilitation blends breathwork, cold immersion, mindfulness, and community to create trauma-informed, heart-centered spaces where people can release, reconnect, and remember who they are.

Amanda Dykann

Yoga + spiritual coach

Amanda Dykann is a yoga teacher, retreat leader, and spiritual coach with nearly 20 years of experience. For over 15 years, she has guided luxury yoga retreats around the world, blending movement, mindfulness, and holistic healing in breathtaking locations.

Trained in Bikram, Vinyasa, and Yin yoga, and holding a master's degree in Spiritual Psychology from the University of Santa Monica, Amanda creates deeply transformative experiences rooted in both yoga and spiritual growth.

Paundi

Internal Family Systems

Paulina and Aundi, PAUNDI, are honored to share the insight and lived experience they have gained through their education and advanced training in Internal Family Systems (IFS). Both are currently pursuing their doctorate degrees in psychology and are Level 3 IFS trained.

Becoming IFS trained in the same year marked a meaningful alignment and the beginning of a shared path in this work. What began as parallel journeys naturally evolved into a shared mission to bring IFS into the world in a way that feels accessible, embodied, relatable, and fun.

Safety

Is this for me?

Great for

  • People in recovery (6+ months sober preferred)
  • Carrying grief, burnout, or a stuck transition
  • Therapists, coaches, healers needing their own container
  • First-timers willing to be guided
  • Couples (only one room available per cohort, ask first)

Please skip if

  • Pregnant (2nd or 3rd trimester)
  • Recent retinal detachment or eye surgery
  • Severe cardiovascular conditions
  • Active psychosis or unmedicated bipolar
  • In acute crisis. Treatment first, retreat after.

All applications include a 30-minute call. We say no when it's not the right time.

Logistics

Common questions.

Where exactly is the retreat?

A private house on the south side of Maui. Full address and directions sent on deposit confirmation.

Which airport do I fly into?

Kahului (OGG). We can arrange transfers from the airport for an additional fee.

Will I have phone service?

Yes, but we ask the group to keep phones off during sessions and meals. Most people leave them in their room by day two.

Dietary needs & allergies?

We accommodate vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and most allergies. Note them on the application.

Refund policy?

Deposit is non-refundable. Full balance refundable up to 45 days out, then 50% refundable up to 14 days out.

Can I come with a partner?

Yes. One shared room available per cohort. Both partners apply separately.

Scholarship for people in recovery?

Yes. One scholarship spot per cohort. Apply with a short letter and a referral from a sponsor or therapist.

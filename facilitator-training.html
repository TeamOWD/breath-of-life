An 8-week hybrid training to become a confident, trauma-sensitive conscious connected breathwork facilitator — online, with an optional in-person practicum on Maui.

The training

What is this?

Learn to hold one-on-one, couples, and group breathwork journeys — live and on Zoom — using somatic attuned touch, energy mapping, and IFS-informed facilitation. Build a practice that is sustainable, trauma-sensitive, and entirely your own.

8 weeks

The curriculum.

Week 1 — Foundations of Self-Led Facilitation

Holding space, self-led presence — calm, clarity, compassion, confidence. Personal "why," breathwork science, journey structure, contraindications and safety. Homework: craft your three-part "why," begin daily breath practice, share guided tracks.

Week 2 — Crafting the Journey: IFS Language & Emotional Mapping

Parts-aware scripting, inviting Self energy, scale of emotions and journey design. Somatic attuned touch with consent. Homework: write a full script, practice touch with a partner, complete a weekly full journey.

Week 3 — Technology, Energy Awareness & Practice

Playlists, group energy dynamics, Zoom facilitation, and recorded practice for feedback. Homework: create your playlist, record and submit a short session.

Week 4 — Business Foundations for Conscious Facilitators

Authentic marketing, equipment and setup, insurance, waivers, scheduling, and client onboarding. Homework: choose insurance, set up scheduling (we use Calendly), draft your marketing plan.

Week 5 — Emotional Intelligence & Facilitator Confidence

Hosting full sessions, using the Scale of Emotions, staying Self-led, and basic chakra and energy awareness. Homework: facilitate a session and refine your facilitation voice.

Week 6 — Community Building & Event Design

Networking, workshop and retreat design, promotional materials, and aligned partnerships. Homework: list 10 community contacts and design your promotional materials.

Week 7 — Live Facilitation & Integration

Promote and host a live Zoom journey, manage bookings and waivers, lead group integration, and receive structured feedback. Homework: host a live journey, continue practice and documentation.

Week 8 — Final Facilitation & Certification

Hold two in-person facilitation sessions and group integration circles. Lead a full in-person journey with instructor observation and co-facilitation at the Breath of Life space. Certification and celebration.

Who this is for

Ready to hold space for others?

This training is for experienced breathworkers, somatic practitioners, coaches, yoga teachers, therapists, and committed beginners ready for deep embodied, trauma-sensitive training.

If you've felt the power of breathwork in your own body and want to learn how to safely guide others through it — this is your next step.

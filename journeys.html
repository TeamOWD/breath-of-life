What to expect

Simple to learn. Powerful to experience.

A little context

You've probably seen terms like Transformational Breathwork, Clarity Breathwork, or Shamanic Breathwork. Different names, mostly the same thing. At its core, this is a modernized form of Pranayama — a practice with a long, deep history in India. We don't believe in overcomplicating it.

How it works

You lie on your back in a comfortable position while music plays around you. Nick guides you through the entire process from start to finish. It's powerful, and it can feel challenging in the first few minutes — but you've done harder things than lying on the floor and breathing.

What you may experience

Deep healing Stress relief Emotional release Inner peace Relief from physical pain Expanded awareness Greater self-love Overwhelming joy Mental clarity Sense of connection Release of grief Deep relaxation

That list probably sounds hard to believe. Good — that's exactly why we want you to come and try it. Breathwork can't really be described. It can only be experienced.

Safety

Is this for me?

Great for

  • First-timers, fully guided
  • People in recovery and the sober community
  • Anxiety, stress, burnout
  • Stuck grief or a life transition
  • Curious and nervous (welcome)

Please skip if

  • Pregnant (2nd or 3rd trimester)
  • Recent retinal detachment or eye surgery
  • Severe cardiovascular conditions
  • Active psychosis or unmedicated bipolar
  • When in doubt, email us first

Common questions

Before your first journey.

Where exactly is it?

Private Maui location. Full address and directions sent on RSVP confirmation.

Can I bring a friend?

Yes. Each RSVP is per person. Have them RSVP separately so we save them a cushion.

What if I cry, panic, or fall asleep?

All normal. We hold the space. Nothing you do in the circle is wrong.

Refund or cancellation?

Contribution based, you pay at the door. Just let us know if you can't make it so we can release your spot.

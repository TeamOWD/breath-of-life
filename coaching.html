Why this program

For the person ready to step into the HIGHEST version of themselves.

If you're ready to stop managing symptoms and start cultivating freedom, this intimate coaching container will help you reconnect with your authentic self through the transformative power of breath, compassionate inquiry, and consistent integration.

This is a high-touch coaching experience for people who are ready to move beyond insight and into integration. Together, we'll combine breathwork, parts work, nervous system regulation, and practical accountability so the transformation you experience becomes the life you actually live.

  • Twice-weekly 1:1 sessions with Nick

How the Journey Unfolds

Profound shifts. By design.

Days 1–14 · Create Safety & Awareness

We begin by slowing down and listening. Together, we'll explore your story, understand your nervous system, identify the parts that have been protecting you, and establish a daily breath practice that becomes your foundation.

Days 15–45 · Release & Reconnect

As safety grows, deeper healing becomes possible. Through breathwork, parts work, and intuitive coaching, you'll begin releasing old burdens and reconnecting with the authentic Self that has always been within you.

Days 46–75 · Embody Lasting Change

Transformation moves beyond the session and into everyday life. We'll strengthen new patterns, deepen your relationships, align your choices with your values, and build the habits that support lasting freedom.

Days 76–90 · Step Into Your Authentic Life

Bring everything together into a way of living that feels aligned, grounded, and true. You'll leave with a personalized practice, a clear vision, and the confidence to live from your authentic Self.

Common questions

Before you apply.

Do I need to be in recovery to apply?

No. Many clients aren't. The work translates to anyone carrying anxiety, grief, or stuck patterns. Recovery context is welcome but not required.

What does a typical week look like?

Two 60 to 90 minute 1:1 sessions, a daily breath protocol you can do in 15 minutes, weekly written reflections, and access to Nick on Voxer between sessions.

Is this therapy?

No. Nick is a certified IFS Informed Coach and breathwork practitioner, not a licensed therapist. Coaching complements clinical care, and many clients work alongside their therapist.

Where do sessions happen?

In-person and Online on the Skool platform.

What's the investment?

Discussed on the discovery call. Premium tier, designed for people ready to invest deeply in themselves and their growth for 90 days.

What if I'm not a fit?

We'll say so on the call and point you to a better starting place, usually the Circle, the book, or a journey.

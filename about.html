Our story

How this started.

Breath of Life Wellness began the way most honest things do. Out of necessity.

Nick TNT got sober at 24, worked the steps, and at year four hit the wall most people don't talk about. He found breathwork, and discovered the piece his recovery had been missing. He went on to write a book modeled on the Big Book of AA, and build a practice on Maui rooted in conscious connected breath, twelve-step wisdom, and the body.

Paulina and Aundi came in with the IFS lens, the parts of us that suffer, protect, manage, and exile. Amanda Dykann brought two decades of yoga and spiritual coaching to every retreat. The team grew; the mission didn't change.

To put a working tool in the hands of anyone who's been trying to feel better and quietly running out of ideas.

The team

Meet the people behind every program.

Nick TNT

Breathwork + recovery

Nick TNT is a breathwork and retreat facilitator with over a decade of experience in wellness and recovery. He specializes in conscious connected breathwork to support emotional release, nervous-system regulation, and reconnection to self-trust and purpose.

He is the founder of Breath of Life Wellness and best-selling author of Breath of Life: Finding Long-Term Recovery with Breathwork and Stepwork. Nick's facilitation blends breathwork, cold immersion, mindfulness, and community to create trauma-informed, heart-centered spaces where people can release, reconnect, and remember who they are.

Amanda Dykann

Yoga + spiritual coach

Amanda Dykann is a yoga teacher, retreat leader, and spiritual coach with nearly 20 years of experience. For over 15 years, she has guided luxury yoga retreats around the world, blending movement, mindfulness, and holistic healing in breathtaking locations.

Trained in Bikram, Vinyasa, and Yin yoga, and holding a master's degree in Spiritual Psychology from the University of Santa Monica, Amanda creates deeply transformative experiences rooted in both yoga and spiritual growth.

Paundi

Internal Family Systems

Paulina and Aundi, PAUNDI, are honored to share the insight and lived experience they have gained through their education and advanced training in Internal Family Systems (IFS). Both are currently pursuing their doctorate degrees in psychology and are Level 3 IFS trained.

Becoming IFS trained in the same year marked a meaningful alignment and the beginning of a shared path in this work. What began as parallel journeys naturally evolved into a shared mission to bring IFS into the world in a way that feels accessible, embodied, relatable, and fun.

About the practice

Common questions.

Are you a treatment center?

No. Breath of Life Wellness is a wellness practice, complementary to clinical treatment, not a substitute for it.

Do I need to be in recovery to work with you?

No. Many of our clients aren't. The work translates to anyone carrying anxiety, grief, or stuck patterns.

Are you licensed therapists?

No. We're certified breathwork practitioners and coaches. We work alongside therapists and refer out when clinical care is the right call.

Where are you based?

Maui, Hawaiʻi. Most in-person work happens here. Online programs run worldwide.

How do I get in touch?

The fastest way is the form on the contact page, or DM us on Instagram. We answer within two business days.

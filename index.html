Honest answers

Common questions.

Where do I sign up for a Breathwork journey on Maui?

We host community breathwork journeys every Friday and Sunday evening in Nick's backyard sanctuary on the south side of Maui. They are contribution-based and open to everyone — no experience needed. Head to our Breathwork Journeys page to RSVP and get the address sent to you directly.

How do I sign up for an online Breathwork experience?

Our online experiences are available through The Circle — a live and on-demand community with weekly guided sessions, a growing library of practices, and members in over thirty countries. You can start with a free seven-day trial, no credit card required. Visit our Journeys page to get started.

What does a Breath of Life retreat look like?

Our retreats are five-day immersive experiences held on Maui in small, intentional groups. Each day includes a morning breathwork session, afternoon integration and parts work, ocean time, cold plunge, hikes, fire circles, and meals shared around one long table. It is designed to take you somewhere you cannot get to in a single session. Visit our Retreats page to see upcoming dates.

Breathwork facilitators training — what is that and how do I do that?

Our Facilitator Training is for anyone who wants to learn how to safely and confidently guide others through breathwork experiences — whether you are a coach, therapist, wellness professional, or simply someone called to share this work. The program covers breathwork mechanics, holding space, contraindications, integration, and the art of facilitation. Reach out through our contact page to learn about the next cohort.

What is the TNT coaching program?

The TNT coaching program is a 90-day private container with Nick. It combines twice-weekly breathwork sessions, parts work, somatic release, and the kind of accountability that does not let you off the hook. It is built for people who are ready to stop managing their pain and start moving through it. Visit our Coaching page to learn more and apply.
